Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026
App: PrepDossier ("the App", "we", "us")
Developer / Data Controller: Alexandru Cristian Băban, Romania
Contact: support@prepdossier.com

This policy explains what personal data we collect when you use the App, why we collect it, and the rights you have over it. We are based in the European Union and comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data we collect

Account data. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name (or a private relay identifier if you use Apple's "Hide My Email"), email address, and an authentication identifier. We use this to create and secure your account.

Content you provide. If you choose to add your CV or background text to personalize a report, we store that text with your account. Providing a CV is entirely optional. We also store the company names, positions, and preferences you enter when creating reports.

Generated content. The interview-preparation reports, quizzes, and quiz results generated for you are stored with your account so you can access them across devices.

Purchase data. Purchases are processed by Apple. We never see or store your payment card details. We receive purchase confirmations (product identifier, transaction identifier, timestamp) through Apple and our payment infrastructure provider (RevenueCat) in order to credit your account.

Usage and diagnostic data. Basic technical data (app version, device type, crash logs, feature usage events) used to keep the App working and improve it. You can turn off usage analytics at any time in Settings → Privacy; diagnostics needed to fix crashes are kept minimal and never include your CV or report content.

Referral data. If you use or share a referral code, we store the code and the connection between referring and referred accounts to grant referral credits and prevent abuse.

2. What we do NOT collect

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, photos, health data, or advertising identifiers. We do not sell personal data. We do not show third-party advertising.

3. How we use your data (and legal bases)

  • Provide the service - account, report generation, library sync. Uses account data, content you provide, and generated content. Legal basis: performance of a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • Personalize report answers to your background - uses your optional CV text. Legal basis: consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) - you may withdraw it at any time by deleting your CV in Settings.
  • Process purchases and credit your account - uses purchase data. Legal basis: performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • Prevent fraud and abuse (e.g. referral fraud, rate limiting) - uses account, usage, and referral data. Legal basis: legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)).
  • Fix bugs and improve the App - uses usage and diagnostic data. Legal basis: legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)).
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, accounting) - uses purchase records. Legal basis: legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)).

4. How report generation works with your data

When you generate a report, the App researches publicly available information about the company and role you specified. Search queries sent to our search providers contain only the company name, position, and related role keywords - never your name, email, or CV. Your CV text (if provided) is transmitted to our AI processing provider (Anthropic) together with the research material solely to generate your personalized report. We have data processing agreements with our providers, and they do not use your data to train their models under our agreements.

5. Who processes your data

We use a small number of service providers ("processors") to run the App:

  • Supabase - database, authentication, and file storage. Your data is hosted in the European Union.
  • Anthropic - AI processing used to generate reports and quizzes (processing may occur in the United States; safeguarded by Standard Contractual Clauses).
  • RevenueCat - purchase validation and entitlement management.
  • Apple - sign-in and payment processing under Apple's own terms.
  • Search providers (e.g. Exa) - receive only company/role search terms, never your identity.

Where data is transferred outside the EU/EEA, we rely on adequacy decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses.

6. Retention

Your account data, CV, reports, and quiz history are kept for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account (Settings → Delete account), all of it - including your CV, generated reports, PDFs, quiz attempts, and referral records - is permanently deleted from our systems. Purchase records may be retained where required by tax and accounting law. Anonymous, aggregated statistics that cannot identify you may be retained.

Cached, non-personal content (such as shared company previews and research-availability records) contains no personal data and is retained independently of any account.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to: access your data; rectify inaccurate data; erase your data; restrict or object to processing; data portability; and withdraw consent at any time (for the CV, simply delete it in Settings). To exercise any right, use the in-app tools or contact us at support@prepdossier.com. We respond within one month.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority - in Romania, the ANSPDCP (Autoritatea Națională de Supraveghere a Prelucrării Datelor cu Caracter Personal, www.dataprotection.ro), or the authority of your country of residence.

8. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and stored on infrastructure that encrypts data at rest at the disk level. Access to user data is restricted by row-level security so that only your authenticated account can access your reports, CV, and credits. Payment card data never touches our systems.

9. Children

The App is not directed at children and is intended for users 16 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be announced in the App before they take effect. The current version is always available here, in the App (Settings → Legal).
